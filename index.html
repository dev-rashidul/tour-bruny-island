- ABOUT THE TOUR

Discover Bruny Island: A Journey of Taste and Tranquility

After being picked up from your cruise ship. hotel or AirBNB in Hobart, we will board on a vehicle ferry for a short journey across the sea channel.

Upon arriving on Bruny Island, you'll head to the Neck, a narrow strip of land that separates the island's northern and southern regions. cheeses, and delicious fresh oysters.

Here, you'll enjoy a picnic-style morning tea featuring warm wood-fired breads, award-winning. Next, you'll take a stroll along a rainforest trail in search of white wallabies as you make your way to the Cape Bruny Lighthouse. You'll have the opportunity to tour the lighthouse and scan the horizon for humpback and southern right whales. Following a lunch made from locally sourced ingredients, you'll visit an oyster farm, a honey producer, a chocolate shop, and the island's cheese company. To burn off some of those calories, you will go on a bush walk led by your guide, who will share insights about Bruny's Island plants and animal life.

Finally, you will return to Hobart with convenient drop-off service at your cruise ship. hotel or AirBNB.
